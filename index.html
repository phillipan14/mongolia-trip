Visa Situation

Student visa expires Jul 10 — mid-trip. Re-entry to China on Jul 14 uses the 240-hour (10-day) visa-free transit for US passports. Requires a confirmed onward ticket to a third country (not Mongolia) within 10 days of arrival. Separate bookings are fine — does not need to be one PNR.
  • Outbound Jul 1: student visa still valid, leave normally.
  • Inbound Jul 14: enter on 240h transit. Show printed PEK → US ticket at immigration.
  • Hotels handle the 24-hour police registration automatically.
  • Wrap up Beijing fully before Jul 1 — apartment, banking, shipping. Don't return with open loops.

Book This Week

  • PEK → ULN flight (Jul 1) and ULN → PEK (Jul 14) — MIAT or Air China. ~$400–700 RT, climbing weekly into Naadam window.
  • PEK → US flight (by ~Jul 20) — required to qualify for transit visa-free. Book before flying to UB.
  • Tour operator — Mongolia DIY outside UB is rough. See list below.
  • Naadam opening ceremony tickets — sell out fast; operator usually bundles.
  • ULN ↔ Dalanzadgad domestic flight — Hunnu Air or Aero Mongolia, limited July seats.

Operators

Nomadic Expeditions

Premium, US-based, long Mongolia track record. Best for hassle-free, high-end. ~$6k+ for two weeks.

Eternal Landscapes

Mid-range, women-led, UB-based. Strong on cultural depth and small groups. ~$3–4.5k.

Stone Horse Expeditions

Horseback-focused, run by long-time Mongolia residents. Ideal if you want serious riding in the steppe.

DIY + local guide

Cheapest route. Hire a UB-based driver-guide directly, ~$80–120/day. More logistics on you.

Things to Flag

  • Naadam window is the busiest week of the year — late April is already late to book.
  • Don't cut the 240h transit close. If your US flight cancels and you can't depart in time, immigration can require same-day exit.
  • Bring printed copies of every booking — phone screens get scrutinized inconsistently.
  • July is high summer, but Gobi nights and Khövsgöl can drop near freezing. Pack layers.
  • Cell coverage outside UB is spotty; download offline maps before leaving.
